首句改寫公式
❌ 懶人換字（扣分）
The line graph illustrates the percentage of households with internet access in four countries between 2000 and 2020.
✅ 結構改寫（得分）
The graph compares the proportion of homes connected to the internet across four nations over a twenty-year period.
換動詞 · 換名詞（percentage→proportion, households→homes）· 換時間說法（between X and Y→over a X-year period）
Overview（整體趨勢）
固定開頭
Overall, an overarching upward trend is evident across all [X] countries.
trend 不能 surge / rise / grow — 用 evident / can be observed
有例外時
Overall, … , with the notable exception of [X], which [did the opposite].
趨勢描述動詞
surge / skyrocket激增（幅度大）
plateau at X%趨於平穩在X%
fluctuate between X and Y在X和Y之間波動
remain relatively stable保持相對穩定
converge markedly by 2020到2020年明顯趨同
比較 / 差距
trail [country] by over 10 percentage points落後X個百分點
marginally higher / lower than略高／低於
markedly higher than顯著高於
approximately X times that of約為X的幾倍
account for X% of佔X%

❌

✅

❌

✅

is projected to reach / hit X%預計達到X%

引言 — 改寫題目 + 立場
立場句（支持）

兩面討論開頭
While … has its merits, I would argue that … ultimately yields greater long-term benefits.
