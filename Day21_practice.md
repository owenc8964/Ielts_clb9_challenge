## 📝 題目

**Some people believe that the best way to reduce crime is to impose longer prison sentences. Others, however, believe there are better alternatives. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.**

## 🧠 CLB 9 骨架建議

```
¶1 引言（2–3句）
   → 改寫題目 + 立場（支持替代方案）
   → 關鍵詞：increasingly prevalent / I contend that...

¶2 延長刑期的支持論點（讓步段）
   → Admittedly, lengthy prison sentences serve as a deterrent...
   → Example: certain categories of violent crime...
   → Link: however, evidence suggests recidivism rates remain high

¶3 替代方案的優勢（主論點）
   → A compelling case can be made that rehabilitation is more effective
   → Example: restorative justice programmes in Norway...
   → Mechanism: This can be attributed to addressing the root causes
   → (可加：social mobility / structural challenges behind crime)

¶4 結論
   → Ultimately, the question is not whether punishment is necessary,
     but whether it alone can build a safer society.
```

## ⚠️ Day 19–20 地雷區 — 本次特別警示

這些是你最近剛犯過的錯，請特別留意：

| ❌ 地雷 | ✅ 正解 | 觸發提醒 |
|---------|---------|---------|
| `widespreadly used` | `widely adopted` | 描述「普遍」用 widely |
| `outweigh than` | `outweigh`（直接接受詞）| 及物動詞不加 than |
| `As the gigantic potential...` | `Given the potential...` | As 後接名詞用 Given |
| `more efficient` | `more effective` | 政策效果用 effective |
| `satisfied to` | `satisfied with` | 固定介系詞 |
| `which using` | `using`（分詞後置）| which 後要有動詞 |
